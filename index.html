What “ZerothNyx” Means

Zerothnyx is a name built in layers. “Zero” reflects the foundation of computing, binary logic, and the idea that creation often begins from nothing — the starting point. “Zeroth” represents origin, the base layer, the first principle before what comes next. “Nyx,” drawn from Greek mythology, represents night — not as emptiness, but as the space where creation begins, where vision forms before it becomes real.

Together, ZerothNyx represents a beginning: the meeting point between technology, imagination, discipline, and future creation.
